Dr. Ben Hamlington

Ben Hamlington is an assistant professor in the Ocean, Earth, and Atmospheric Sciences Department at Old Dominion University. Prior to arriving at ODU, Ben obtained his BS and MS in Mechanical Engineering from Washington University in St. Louis, and his PhD in Aerospace Engineering Sciences from the University of Colorado at Boulder. His primary research interest is on past, present and future sea level rise, focusing on the use of satellite observations to improve our understanding of the ocean. Beyond studying sea level, Ben does research on a wide range of topics including ENSO/climate variability, tsunamis, wildfires, and land subsidence.

Lauren Sommers, M.S.
