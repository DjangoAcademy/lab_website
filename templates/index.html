Welcome

Welcome to the webpage for the Ocean Remote Sensing Group at Old Dominion University. We are part of the Center for Coastal Physical Oceanography in the Ocean, Earth and Atmospheric Sciences Department.

Our research focus is on past, present and future sea level rise, specializing in the use of satellite observations to improve the understanding of the ocean. On this page, we will provide updates on group activities, including recently published papers.

Please feel free to get in touch with any questions, or if you are interested in joining our group.
