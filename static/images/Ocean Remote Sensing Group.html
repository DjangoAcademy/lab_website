  • About

    The Ocean Remote Sensing Group at Old Dominion University studies a wide range of research topics including sea level rise, ocean/climate variability, natural hazards and climate change. The primary focus is on the use of satellite remote sensing to improve our understanding of the ocean in the past, present and future. While the group is part of the Ocean, Earth and Atmospheric Sciences Department at ODU, our expertise extends beyond oceanography to include aerospace, civil and mechanical engineering and applied mathematics.
